Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions.

How can I volunteer?

Fill out our contact form selecting "Volunteer Application" or visit our Community page to see available opportunities. We'll contact you within 48 hours.

Are your programs free?

Yes! Most of our programs, including health screenings, workshops, and support groups, are free. Some specialized programs may have nominal fees.

How do I register for events?

Use the contact form with "Program Registration" subject, or visit our Programs page for specific event registration links.

Do you offer online programs?

Yes! Many of our workshops and support groups are available online. Check our Programs page for virtual options.

How can my mosque partner with you?

We welcome mosque partnerships! Contact us with "Partnership Opportunity" and we'll discuss how to bring our programs to your community.

Is mental health support confidential?

Absolutely. All support group discussions and counseling sessions are completely confidential. Your privacy is our priority.
